Optique Echouate

Production-grade e-commerce platform purpose-built for optical retail

Overview

Optique Echouate is a full-featured e-commerce platform designed specifically for optical retail businesses. It enables seamless management of optical products — including glasses, lenses, and accessories — with a comprehensive backend system for inventory, orders, and customer relationships.

Built with Laravel on the backend and React on the frontend, the platform delivers a modern shopping experience with real-time stock updates, an intuitive admin dashboard, and a full checkout workflow. The project demonstrates end-to-end full-stack development, from database schema design to responsive UI implementation.
